Culture and Tradition of the State of Assam – Assam Culture And Tradition The culture of this state is rich, it is the meeting place of South Asian and South East Asian culture, where the main language is Assamese. The population settlement of the state also includes people from different tribes who have their own independent communities and beliefs, here you get to see the mixed culture and lifestyle, in which the Assamese tradition from ancient times along with the beliefs and traditions of the existing tribes. The people here follow. In the Egyptian culture, the mixed influence of Mongolian, India, Brahmadesh, Iranian and Aryan way of life is more visible on the lives of the people here, and according to that, the dialects, costumes, festivals, music, dance, food etc. Happens. Overall, the Indian state of Assam has maintained its own independent identity and way of life. The design of dance, music, musical instruments and clothes of Assam is also slightly different than other states. Music and Choreography of the State of Assam – Music and Folk Dance in Assam Due to being inhabited by most of the tribal people in the state, the mixed lifestyle is found here, in which along with classical music, songs of the beliefs and traditions of the tribes are also present. Folk songs are very much prevalent in Assam, which include things related to the common lifestyle of rural departments and their description. Tribal tribes have their own beliefs about forests, trees, plants, animals, vegetation, moon, sun, stars, which are described through their folk songs, and their festivals, customs, songs of rites are also included in folk songs. Happens. There are more than hundreds of tribes living in Assam and adjoining states, whose influence on music and songs is seen among themselves. Artifacts of literature are also brought here directly by songs, in which Assamese literature songs produced from the earlier times, till today, people here like to listen and sing with pleasure. The main songs included in his songs are Bihu Geet, Shram Geet, Nichu Koni Geet, Dhai Naam Lullaby, Deh Vichar Geet, Dev – Devi Geet, Soot Cutting Geet, Fishing Songs, Malita, Twelve Month Songs, Frogi There are famous song types like Wedding Songs, Oja Pali Songs, Wedding Songs, Apsari, Religious, Paddy Songs etc. Talking about dance, here Bihu dance, Bagrumba dance, Jhumre dance, Devdhani, Bhortal, Ali A ligang dance, Ankiya Nat, Khamba Lim etc. are famous dance types. Assam State People's Costume – Dress / Costume of Assam State Peoples Like other states of India, the state of Assam also has its own independent identity, in which this uniqueness is clearly reflected in Assam in various ways. Talking about the costume, the main dress of the men living in the state is Gamsa and Dhoti, which is the traditional dress of Assam. In this era of modernity, bets, t-shirts, pants, jeans pants, etc. are commonly used in men's apparel. The main dress of the women of Assam includes sarees, makhela chadar etc., nowadays the use of sarees has become common in the clothes of tribal women. Makhela and Chadar are the traditional dress of women in Assam, which you can easily see worn by these women on special occasions. Staple Food of Assam: Rice is used in abundance in the main food type of people of almost all the states under this state, here you will also get to see different types of rice. People who are fond of eating fish with rice are found in most quantity in the state, people of the state prefer to eat less spicy dishes. Fruits are mixed with exotic herbs in large quantities in the cuisine, chira and curd are commonly used here for breakfast. You will find most of the food types of this state prepared with the help of rice, which include Pitha, Lao Pani etc., Masor Tenga, Laska Stock, Pedus Pomfret, Bamboo Fish etc. Delicious fish dishes are also eaten here with great fervor. . The dishes like Khar, Aloo Pitika, Eri Polu etc. are also included in the food here, in this 'Khar' which is a delicious type made from banana fruit is a special identity of this state.
