Hinduism is the main religion for the people of Sikkim although a large population also practices Buddhism. The Tibetans and people of Sikkim, and the people of Bhutan follow Buddhism. Along with Hindu festivals, Nepali festivals are also followed here. The major festivals of Sikkim are Drukpresi, Pang Lubasol, Saga Dawa, Losong and Dasain are widely celebrated here. Among the Hindu festivals celebrated mainly are Durga Puja, Dussehra and Diwali.

Losar is the main festival of Sikkim. Which is celebrated by the people of the state. It is celebrated as the Tibetan New Year. In which everyone congratulates each other. And share happiness. And live with mutual brother fodder. Relatives are called. And congratulations are given. A banquet is organized in which all the people live together. This festival is celebrated according to Buddhism. In which special types of religious rituals are performed. And the ancestors are remembered.

Saga Festival is a major religious festival celebrated in Sikkim. It is a holy month for the people of Buddhism to pray in the monasteries. Which is known as Buddha Purnima all over India. This festival is dedicated to Lord Buddha. It is believed. Lord Buddha was born on this day. In this festival, information about Lord Buddha is given. Sacred texts are read. Sobha Yatra is taken out. This festival is celebrated with great pomp inside Sikkim.

The festival of Drukpa Teshi is celebrated in Sikkim. It is mostly celebrated by the people of Buddhism. There are many mythological beliefs behind this festival. In this, Lord Buddha gave the first sermon at Sarnath to his four disciples. This festival is celebrated in his honor. There is a lot of recognition of Drukpa Teshi in the people of Buddhism. It is celebrated on the fourth day of the 6th month according to the Tibetan calendar.

Tamu Losar also figures prominently in the festival celebrated in Sikkim. Which is celebrated by the Gurung community in the northeastern states and many countries. This festival is celebrated as New Year. In which people congratulate each other and give good wishes. Various types of competitions, dance music are organized in this festival. in which the local people participate. Fairs are held at many places in the state. In which traditional things and handicrafts are put on the stalls here. Which you can also buy.

Sakewa Festival is the main festival celebrated in Sikkim. Which is celebrated by the Kirat Khambu Rai community of Sikkim. In which many types of religious rituals are performed. And Mother Earth is worshipped. In which drums are played which reverberate throughout the area. This community has been settled here since very ancient times. The people of this community are natural worshippers. This festival is celebrated for the good of all. So that everyone may be happy. This festival is celebrated mutually. Many types of religious and traditional worship are performed on this day. This festival is celebrated for 9 days. This festival begins on the full moon day in the Hindu month of Vaishakh. In which all the people gather in one place. Worship is done there. After which people go to their homes. and perform sacred rites for the ancestors.
